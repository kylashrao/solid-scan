About SolidScan

SolidScan is a high-performance, client-side document utility designed to bridge the gap between premium mobile application experiences and standard desktop web browsers.

Our Mission

We built SolidScan for professionals, students, and everyday users who need to digitize physical documents without compromising their data privacy. Most free online PDF converters force you to upload your sensitive files to their remote servers, creating a massive security vulnerability.

By leveraging advanced HTML5 Canvas technology, SolidScan processes everything locally on your own device. Your financial, legal, and personal documents never touch a remote server, ensuring total privacy.
