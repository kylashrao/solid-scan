Privacy Policy

At SolidScan, your privacy is our absolute priority. We designed this application specifically to eliminate the security risks associated with traditional online PDF converters.

When you use SolidScan to capture images or convert files to PDF, 100% of the processing happens locally within your device's web browser memory. We do not upload, store, or transmit your documents, images, or resulting PDFs to any external servers.

Analytics
